Our Mission

Many shelters deal with the dual problems of little money and low visibility in their areas as the number of animals in shelters keeps growing. The goal of this project is to develop an easy-to-use website that highlights nearby animal shelters, the creatures they have up for adoption, and the advantages of pet adoption. The website can help foster relationships between shelters and potential pet owners by offering a consolidated resource for pet adoption, which would ultimately boost adoption rates and enhance animal welfare.
